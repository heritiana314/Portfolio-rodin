Portfolio Details

SUB/VAC

DESCRIPTION

   SUB/VAC is a web application designed to streamline the verification process of teacher's allowances and subsidies within the Vakinankaratra region. It provides a centralized platform for managing and tracking financial transactions related to educators, ensuring transparency and efficiency.

  • Key Features:

    • Centralized Data Management: SUBVAC offers a single location to store and manage information about teacher's allowances and subsidies

    Verification Process: The application facilitates the verification of financial transactions, ensuring accuracy and accountability

    Transparency and Efficiency: SUBVAC promotes transparency in the distribution of funds and streamlines the overall process, saving time and resources

  • Objective:

    •    SUB/VAC aims to improve the financial management of teacher's allowances and subsidies, benefiting both educators and administrators in the Vakinankaratra region
